MIT SWE hosts this annual design competition which pits teams of 4-5 students against each other in an intense battle of quick designing and thinking. BeaverDash is an opportunity for local high school students to learn about practical engineering techniques while practicing innovative and creative thinking. An MIT undergraduate student is assigned to each team to mentor and guide the teams while raising awareness of engineering fields and disciplines.


The next BeaverDash competition will be held in April 2010.
